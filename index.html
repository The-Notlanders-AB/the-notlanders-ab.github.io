Fail Fail Succeed

Wishlist now!

Fail Fail Succeed is a 2D puzzle platformer where failing isn't just a part of the game; it's a strategic move towards success.

You're an anonymous individual lost and trapped inside a dream-like dimension, with little to no idea of how to get out.

But something has changed, and from somewhere a dog has appeared. It seems to want to guide you forward. There's one problem though the path forward is full of deadly traps… and your one and only ability? To fail.

Fortunately for you, there are mysterious orbs dotted around the environment that change what it is you leave behind when you fail… and with that, all you've got to do is fail, fail again, and eventually, if you don't give up, you will be able to make it out of this place.
