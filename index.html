A micro-indie studio in Sweden

Who are we?

We're a team of two, handcrafting tiny worlds with big souls.
With a small team, each and every game is a personal love letter to the power of play. No cookie-cutter mechanics, just clever and thoughtful designs, with playful graphics and personal narratives.

Our goal is to make games, not to make money and earn fame, but rather out of the love for making them and the benefit of the community of fellow gamers and developers. We hope not only to be able to provide quality games, but also be a source of information, inspiration and support.
